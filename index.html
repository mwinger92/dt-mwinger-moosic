We are Moosic.

The world's first Moose Rock band. Think of your favorite soft rock indie band, and then think of the mighty North American Moose. We aren't mooses (Meese?), personally. But we're all from different places where Moose can be found. Our drummer's from Alaska. Our Singer's from Northen Minnesota. Our bassist is from Manitoba. We make music for those who are powerful but calm, and love the cold.
